FACILITIES

Hotel Vrinda has all the modern day amenities on hand that one would need during their stay. Our ever smiling and humble staff will always be at your service for your utmost comfortable stay in Nathdwara.

AIRPORT PICKUP (CHARGEABLE)

Arriving by plane and need someone to pick you up to the hotel? We have got it covered for you, just inform us in advance.

RESTAURANT

Plunge into one of the best restaurants in Nathdwara that serves the most delicious and sumptuous cuisines and delight your taste buds.

WELCOME DRINK ON ARRIVAL

Guests must be tired due to a long journey and a refreshing non alcoholic drink will delight their mood and day as well.

DOCTOR ON CALL

Feeling low or unwell and doesn't know what to do or need some medical advice? Inform us, we have doctors on call for your time in need.

NEWS PAPER (COMMON AREA)

In our common area we have newspapers and magazines for our guests because we know many of you have a habit of that.

FIRE EXTINGUISHER

In case of emergencies like a fire burst we have enough number of fire extinguishers to control the situation until the fire brigade comes.

LAUNDRY (CHARGEABLE)

We also provide laundry service to our guests. You will get the clean clothes the next day you give it for laundry.

SECURITY

There is no reason to feel unsafe when you are staying at Hotel Vrinda because we have all the security measures to keep you safe.

24 X 7 ROOM SERVICE

You can contact our staff or front desk at any hour of time. They will always be on their toes to help you out.

DAILY HOUSEKEEPING

Our daily housekeeping staff keeps the property as well as your rooms / washrooms neat and clean.
