WELCOME TO HOTEL VRINDA

Nestled amidst the Aravalli Hills, the charming town of Nathdwara literally translates to "The Gateway of the Lord". It is also a sacred Hindu site and is flooded with thousands of tourists every year. It is situated in the heart of Rajasthan, and is a pristine picturesque location. Nathdwara is also famous for "Pichwai Paintings" which are traditional terracotta work and Rajasthani style paintings. It is considered the hub of traditional handicrafts, arts and artefacts.

We have been a well known hotel in Nathdwara near Shrinathji temple - “Hotel Vrinda” since 1983. And recently we have added two more family hotel in Nathdwara; Hotel Dwarika by Vrinda & Hotel Shreenath by Vrinda. All our hotels offer comfortable and rejuvenating stay along with freshly prepared delicious food in our on-site restaurant in Nathdwara - Annakut which is very famous for Gujarati thali. We also have another restaurant for à la carte - Sakhdi - A multi cuisine restaurant. Here you can relish a variety of food dishes with your family & friends.
